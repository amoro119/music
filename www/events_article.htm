DVD Release Concert « Ensemble M&M » featuring Kae Shiraki
27/03/2011 20:30

Grand Auditorium, Conservatoire de Luxembourg

After : read more on a)Events and b) News

Ensemble M&M was founded in 2008. Ever since the double father and son duo- Jean and Gary Muller, Michel and Paul Mootz - has experienced growing success. In 2010 they engaged in the production of DVD that will be presented to the Luxembourgish public at a concert in the Conservatoire de Luxembourg on March 27, 2011. The program includes works by Gershwin, Bernstein, Rachmaninov and as a special highlight the world premiere of an arrangement of John Williams’ "Star Wars Suite" by John Williams, arranged by Jan Valta.
